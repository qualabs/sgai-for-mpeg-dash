## Accepted ad-type values

The IAB doc enumerates the **CTV Ad Portfolio**: Linear Ad, Pause Ad,
Menu Ad, Squeezeback, Overlay, In Scene Ads, Screensaver Ad. The
Linear Ad entry is the traditional in-stream pod slot and is not
broken down further at the format-name level; the six non-linear /
hybrid additions are the focus of the 2026 update.

| Name | Category | Visual placement | Typical duration / interaction | Source paragraph / heading |
|------|----------|------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------|
| Linear Ad | Linear (in-stream) | Full-screen ad takes over the player; pre-roll, mid-roll, or post-roll. | 6/15/20/30 s (3-10 s bumpers and 60 s also accepted; info-mercials 1-5 min with skip option). Interactive ads 15-30 s compulsory, indefinite on interaction. | Linear Ad Format Guidelines |
| Pause Ad | Non-linear, viewer-initiated | Full-screen (1920x1080, 16:9) or partial-screen (600x600, 1:1) over the paused frame. | Display/Static (file weight under 350 kB) or Video/Animated. Ends on dismiss / resume / app exit / power off / screensaver. Audio not required by default. | Pause Ad |
| Menu Ad | Non-linear, OS/UI-integrated | Headline Banner (2:3, 6:5, or 16:9) across a row in the navigation UI; In-Menu Tile (16:9 or 3:9) anywhere in the menu. | Display/Static or Video/Animated. Duration tied to viewer behaviour in the menu; some refresh, some are part of navigable carousels. | Menu Ad |
| Squeezeback | Non-linear, alongside-content | L-Shape (content squeezed to upper-left/right, ad across bottom + right bar); Frame (content centred, ad surrounds); Double Box Video (content left, ad right, each 25%); Double Box Video + Background (same with branded background). Each 1920x1080 frame, 16:9. | Display/Static or Video/Animated. Minimum 10 s, duration signalled in bid request. Content reduction takes 1-2 s. | Squeezeback |
| Overlay | Non-linear, over-content | Corner Overlay (25% of 1920x1080, one of four corners) or Lower Third Overlay (30% of bottom of 1920x1080). 16:9. | Display/Static or Video/Animated. Minimum 10 s. Audio not required by default. | Overlay |
| In Scene Ads | Non-linear, composited into content | Composited inside the scene as a billboard, virtual out-of-home placement, or product placement. Multiple aspect ratios: 9:16 (1080x1920), 4:3 (1280x960), 16:9 (1920x1080), Poster (840x400), Bulletin (1400x400). | Static image (jpg, png, gif); 3D/CGI by partner agreement. Minimum 3 s Brand Exposure Duration. No interactivity (brand placement). | In Scene Ads |
| Screensaver Ad | Non-linear, OS/App-initiated | Full-screen 1920x1080, 16:9, after a period of device inactivity. | Display/Static or Video/Animated. Ends on dismiss / resume / app exit / device timeout. Some refresh; videos may autoplay. | Screen Saver Ad |

## Mapping to norm chapter 3

Chapter 3 of the norm (Terms, definitions, abbreviations) carries a
normative list of accepted ad-type values. Each value below is the
canonical IAB name verbatim; the norm MUST NOT invent new names
beyond this list (R12).

| IAB ad-type | Norm chapter 3 identifier | Rationale (1 line) |
|-------------|---------------------------|--------------------|
| Linear Ad | `linear-ad` | Existing DASH SGAI baseline (`InsertPresentation` / `ReplacePresentation`); already covered by MPEG-DASH 6th edition. |
| Pause Ad | `pause-ad` | Viewer-initiated overlay anchored to a Broadcaster-declared window of validity — UC-05. |
| Menu Ad | `menu-ad` | Out of scope for the foundation phase of this norm (UI-integrated, not driven by an MPD event in the primary timeline). Listed for completeness; not addressed in chapters 4-7. |
| Squeezeback | `squeezeback` | Layout variants (L-Shape, Frame, Double-Box, Double-Box + Background) MUST be encoded as the layout vocabulary on `@allowedLayouts`, not as new ad-types — the type is one. |
| Overlay | `overlay` | Compositing ad on top of content while it plays — UC-03. Sub-positions (Corner, Lower-Third) are layout values, not new types. |
| In Scene Ads | `in-scene-ad` | Out of scope for the foundation phase (requires content-side compositing, not a Player-managed surface). Listed for completeness; not addressed in chapters 4-7. |
| Screensaver Ad | `screensaver-ad` | OS-initiated, fires outside the playback session. Out of scope for the foundation phase — listed for completeness. |

Layout sub-values (the spatial placements inside an overlay or
squeezeback) MUST be carried on the `@allowedLayouts` attribute of
the SGAI slot, using the IAB-canonical names verbatim
(`corner-overlay`, `lower-third-overlay`, `l-shape`, `frame`,
`double-box`, `double-box-background`, `fullscreen`, `partial-screen`).
Position semantics inside a layout (left vs right corner, exact pixel
offsets) are delegated to HTML5/CSS per R10.

## Notes

- The IAB 2026 release removed the legacy non-linear submission
  guidelines block and redirected legacy "Instream" references to
  OpenRTB AdCOM `plcmt` subtypes. SGAI consumers of this catalogue
  should pin to the May 2026 release.
- Interactivity inside an ad surface (QR codes, SIMID-based rich
  interactions) is **inside** the ad creative and **out of scope for
  the SGAI norm**. The norm cares about *when* and *which* ad surface
  is allowed; what the creative does inside that surface is a SIMID /
  VAST application-layer concern.
- The IAB doc anchors VAST 4.2+ as the wire for interactive ads and
  flags SIMID as the replacement for VPAID. SGAI is VAST-agnostic per
  R11; this is informative context, not a normative dependency.
