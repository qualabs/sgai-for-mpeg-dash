## Open questions surfaced by v5 (informative only)

The Open-questions-for-WG section added to the spec body in v5
(after Refinement gaps) is the new artefact of this iteration.
It is informative; none of the OQ-1..OQ-12 items has been applied
to the normative chapters. The list maps each open question to:
(a) the issue body in the `qualabs/sgai-for-mpeg-dash` issue
tracker proposal (Issues #2..#13), (b) the section(s) in this
spec that will need editing once the WG decides, (c) the
provenance in the WG meeting transcript (Tactiq doc fileId
`1SvawimhYGJ4NCYImyex1KkkzwB6r7GcDMqn9rU2X3zs`).

The validator does NOT report OQ-1..OQ-12 as gaps or ambiguities
in the count above because:

- OQ-N items are externally surfaced design questions, not gaps
  in the current spec's internal coverage of `context/`.
- The spec preserves the current `context/03-requirements.md`
  formulations (R10, R14, R3) faithfully; the WG questions are
  proposals to change requirements, not statements that the spec
  has implemented them incorrectly.
- Treating WG-surfaced open questions as gaps would conflate
  "spec disagrees with `context/`" (a gap) with "WG may amend
  `context/` later" (a future-major signal). The former requires
  a fix; the latter is just informational.

Validator's read on the open-questions section: **safe and
appropriate** for a minor / additive iteration. The section
clearly marks itself informative and explicitly states none of
the items is in scope for v5's normative chapters. Two stylistic
notes (not blocking, not flagged as ambiguities):

- The OQ numbering (OQ-1..OQ-12) starts at 1; the GitHub-issue
  numbering (Issues #2..#13) starts at 2. The skipped Issue #1
  is documented in the issue proposal (removed by Nicolas on
  2026-05-20). The spec OQ table is self-consistent; if a reader
  comes from the GitHub issue tracker, the OQ-to-Issue mapping
  resolves the offset clearly.
- The placeholder framing "issue numbers are placeholders until
  creation" admits the issues do not yet exist. Once the issues
  are created in `qualabs/sgai-for-mpeg-dash` with their actual
  numbers, the OQ table benefits from a minor update to remove
  the "placeholders" qualifier.
