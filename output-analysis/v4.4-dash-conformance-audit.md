# DASH conformance audit — v6 (2026-05-20)

**Audit target**: `../output/v6-sgai-spec.md`.
**Reference**: MPEG-DASH 6th edition (ISO/IEC 23009-1:2025, FDIS),
consulted via NotebookLM notebook "Streaming Protocols — DASH,
HLS, C2PA, DRM" — but in this run NO new NotebookLM queries were
issued. The v4.2 audit's Q1/Q2 grounding (verbatim §5.16.5 unit
declaration; `<xs:element>` absence for `UrlParamInfo` /
`RequestParam` in the printed schema) covers every construct that
v6 carries over. v6 introduces NO new constructs.

**Method**: inventory of new / modified constructs introduced by
the spec, per-construct verdict (Conforming `V` / Marginal `M` /
Non-conforming `N`).

## Scope

v6 is a minor-style additive iteration on top of v5. The XML
construct surface is byte-identical to v5 (no new schemes, no
new attributes, no new elements, no annex example changed). v6
only changes prose in three places:

- §1.1 enhancement-layer transparent video overlays OOS bullet
  (tightening — closes v5 detail-review flag-3).
- "Open questions for WG (2026-06-02 meeting)" section: OQ-3
  origin-cell rewording (closes v5 detail-review flag-1) and a
  new lifecycle framing paragraph (addresses v5 detail-review
  "Open questions surfaced" stylistic note 1).
- Version-line bump (v5 -> v6).

The audit therefore carries every verdict forward unchanged from
v5. The construct inventory below restates the v5 audit's
C-01..C-28 with the line-number anchors updated to v6 where the
prose moved. The audit registers NO new findings.

This audit is conformance-against-DASH-6th only. Design-level
audit lives in `v6-spec-validation.md`; detail review lives in
`v6-detail-review.md`.

## NotebookLM findings (highlights)

No fresh NotebookLM query was issued in this audit. The v4.2
audit's Q1 (verbatim §5.16.5 grounding) and Q2 (printed-schema

## Non-conforming items detail

### N-1: Overlay Resolution Document has zero `<Period>` elements (C-20)

**Status: carried forward unchanged from v5 audit.** v6
preserves the §5.2.2 boilerplate, Annex C.3, and Annex E.3 verbatim
from v5. The Refinement-gaps section row 1 still tags this as a
context-level architectural decision deferred to the next major
build. The cleanest remedy proposed in v4.2 (Period-wrapper
variant) still applies; the choice is unchanged in v6.

## Marginal items detail

### M-1: `@url` vs `@uri` on `<InsertPresentation>` / `<ReplacePresentation>` (C-14, C-15)

**Status: carried forward unchanged from v5 audit.** v6
preserves the v5 inline NOTEs verbatim. The trade-off
(prose-fidelity vs schema-fidelity) is unchanged.

### M-2: `@earliestResolutionTimeOffset` rebound unit on SVTA elements (C-13)

**Status: carried forward unchanged from v5 audit.** v6
preserves the §5.1.3 attribute-table disambiguation paragraph and
the §5.1.2 G-7 NOTE. Refinement-gaps row 2 still applies.

### M-3: `urn:svta:dash:sgai-layout:2026` declared but never used — resolved in v4.1, still absent in v6

**Status: still resolved.** v6 §2.1 carries no row for
`urn:svta:dash:sgai-layout:2026`. M-3 remains closed.

### M-4: Baseline DASH elements re-used inside foreign-namespace subtrees (C-17, C-19)

**Status: carried forward unchanged from v5 audit.** v6
preserves the §5.3.4 and §5.5.3 NOTEs. Refinement-gaps row 15
still applies (context-level authorisation pending). v6's §1.1
enhancement-layer OOS bullet rewrite is consistent with the M-4
posture for C-17 — the sub-MPD reached via `<ImportedMPD>` is
already permitted to carry any Representation whose `@mimeType`
is RFC 4337-compliant; admitting scalable-codec Representations
inside that sub-MPD is the existing degree of freedom under
§5.3.4, made explicit in v6 to remove the ambiguity the v5
detail-review flag-3 identified.

### M-5: `<UrlParamInfo>` vs `<RequestParam>` element name (C-28)

**Status: carried forward unchanged from v5 audit.** v6 §2 and
§5.7 mitigations are unchanged from v5.

## Open questions surfaced

- The Open-questions-for-WG section in v6 is **outside** the
  conformance audit's surface: it is informative material, and
  none of the OQ-1..OQ-12 items mutates a normative construct in
  v6. The audit does NOT carry an open question per OQ — they
  are tracked in `v6-spec-validation.md` as informative-only. The
  new lifecycle paragraph added in v6 is meta-information about
  the section's status across future editions; it introduces no
  XML construct. The OQ-3 cell rewording (v6 delta) is editorial
  inside an informative section.
- The conformance audit's open-question list is therefore
  unchanged from v5: the published DASH-MPD.xsd's authoritative
  declaration of `UrlParamInfo` / `RequestParam` is still outside
  the corpus available to NotebookLM (carries from v4.2 Q2
  finding); the §5.16.5 "60s" default-value prose discrepancy is
  still an inherited DASH 6th oddity (carries from v4.2 Q1
  finding); and the N-1 remedy variants are still three
  (Period-wrapper recommended) carrying from v4.1.
- If/when the WG resolves OQ-4 (DynamicPresentation event) or
  OQ-5 (concurrent overlay carrier shape), the conformance audit
  will need a fresh NotebookLM query against DASH 6th to verify
  the new construct's placement and ignore-if-unknown semantics.
  v6 does not require that query because v6 carries no new
  construct.
