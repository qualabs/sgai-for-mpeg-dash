## Open questions surfaced

- The `<EventStream>@value` attribute is present on the §5.5.1
  "carrier shape" canonical example (L:1099, `value="1"`) and on
  every sub-MPD-embedded callback `<EventStream>` (Annex A.4
  L:1786-1787, Annex C.4 L:2013), but is omitted from every
  `<EventStream>` placed as a child of `<svta:RenderableAsset>`
  (the §5.5.3 example at L:1151 and Annexes C, E inline carriers).
  The split mirrors a real DASH 6th distinction (sub-MPD
  `<EventStream>` as a `<Period>` child vs SGAI `<EventStream>`
  reinterpreted under `<svta:RenderableAsset>`), but the spec
  does not say so anywhere. Worth a one-sentence NOTE in §5.5.3
  in a future build clarifying whether `@value` is intentionally
  optional in the SGAI placement.
