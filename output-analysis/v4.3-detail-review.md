## Open questions surfaced

- The "Open questions for WG (2026-06-02 meeting)" section is the
  first material extension this spec carries beyond its chapter-1
  scope; future minor refinements should clarify whether the
  section is permanent (a living index of WG state across all
  editions) or transient (deleted once the WG decides). The current
  v5 framing reads as transient — the section is dated and refers
  to the specific 2026-06-02 meeting — but the spec body does not
  state the section's lifecycle. A reader picking up v5 a year
  from now may not know whether the listed OQs are still open.
  Worth a one-sentence framing note in a future refinement (e.g.
  "This section is dropped from the spec once the corresponding
  WG decisions are recorded in `.project/decisions/` ADRs.").
- The OQ table cell "Origin in this spec" varies in detail across
  rows. Some cells point at a specific subsection (e.g. OQ-1's
  `§1.1 layout OOS bullet; §3.2 layout vocabulary`); others are
  more abstract (OQ-9 / OQ-10 / OQ-11 say `n/a (process item)`).
  The inconsistency is not a correctness issue, but a reader
  expecting uniform information density across the rows will
  notice it. Future minor refinements MAY normalise the column.
